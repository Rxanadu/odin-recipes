Fruit Extract

    Ingredients
  • Fruit - 2 1/2 cups
  • (optional) Sugar - 3 tbsp
  • Water - 4 1/2 cups
  • Container
    Kitchenware
  • Large pot
  • Colander
  • Large bowl

    Instructions
  1. Rinse off any dirt from fruits
  2. Place fruit and water into pot
  3. Place heat to medium-high and let heat up until boiling
  4. Heat up fruits for 15-20 minutes
  5. Taste test fruit extract. If extract tastes bitter, place up to the full amount of sugar into extact - until extract is to your liking
  6. Place colander on top of large bowl
  7. Turn off heat and transfer extract to colander
  8. Let sit for ~40 minutes or until cool
  9. Transfer extract to container and place in fridge to cool
  10. Enjoy!
