Sweet Oatmeal

    Ingredients
  • Oats - 8 tbsp
  • Brown Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Ground Cinnamon - 1/2 tsp
  • Milk - 1/4 cup

If you have no milk, replace it with water


    Instructions
  1. Place milk in pot and heat until simmering
  2. Place oats, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon into pot
  3. Stir ingrdients until sugar and cinnamon are mixed together
  4. Heat for 5 minutes or until oats become soft and dark in color. Periodically stir oatmeal to prevent sticking to bottom
  5. Turn off heat and transfer to bowl
  6. Let sit for 1-2 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
