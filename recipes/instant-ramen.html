Instant Ramen

    Ingredients
  • Instant Ramen - 1 packet
  • Water - 2 1/2 cups
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Garlic (crushed, diced) - 1-2 cloves
  • Onion, sliced - 2 tbsp
  • (optional) Green onions - bottom, sliced - 1 tbsp
  • (optional) Green onions - top, sliced - 1/2 tbsp
    Kitchenware
  • Large pot
  • Colander
  • Large bowl

    Instructions
  1. Place onions, garlic, salt, and water into pot
  2. Place heat to medium-high and let heat up until boiling
  3. Cook for 20-25 min minutes. Add additional salt for taste
  4. Place colander on top of large bowl
  5. Turn off heat and transfer extract to colander
  6. Let sit for ~40 minutes or until cool. This allows the moisture from onion and garlic to drain into stock
  7. Place stock into pot
  8. Set heat to medium-high and bring stock to a boil
  9. Place instant ramen noodles and bottom green onions into pot. Do NOT use soup base included in packet!
  10. Cook noodles for 7-10 minutes or until soft
  11. Transfer ramen into dinner bowl
  12. Garnish ramen with top green onions
  13. Enjoy!
